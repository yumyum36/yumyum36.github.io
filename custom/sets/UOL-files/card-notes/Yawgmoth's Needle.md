In CC12 R4, the theme of the round was to make a cross between two strong cards without the end result being broken. This card was a cross between [Yawgmoth's Will and Pithing Needle.](https://scryfall.com/search?q=%22yawgmoth%27s+will%22+or+%22pithing+needle%22&unique=cards&as=grid)

During the course of a competition a player may fail to submit. The organizer may sometimes create a substitute card, with the initials "DNS" in the card name. (DNS is short for Did Not Submit)
