In CC12 R4, the theme of the round was to make a cross between two strong cards without the end result being broken. This card was a cross between [High Tide and Skullclamp](https://scryfall.com/search?q=%22High+Tide%22+or+skullclamp&unique=cards&as=grid)

During the course of a competition a player may fail to submit. The organizer may sometimes create a substitute card, with the initials "DNS" in the card name. (DNS is short for Did Not Submit)
